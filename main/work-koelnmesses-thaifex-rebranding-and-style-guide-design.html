Koelnmesse’s THAIFEX Rebranding and Style-guide Design

Brief

For over a decade, THAIFEX - World of Food Asia has retained its brand identity and appearance. Understanding the growing importance of branding, THAIFEX wanted to refresh their identity and update it with today’s design trends.

Solution

Through careful consideration of the existing brand image, the team wanted to maintain the integrity and identification of the brand. Inspired by the nature of THAIFEX, we partnered up with THAIFEX’s design team to deliver an all-new identifiable key visual that could apply itself consistently across any array of assets. Along with the new key visual, a contemporary set of brand colours, as well as appropriate brand fronts, were stylishly selected for the revamped THAIFEX.
