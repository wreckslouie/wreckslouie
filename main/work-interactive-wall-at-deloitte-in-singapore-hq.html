Installation of Traditional-Digital Hybrid Interactive Wall With CMS Integration

Interactive Wall at Deloitte in Singapore HQ

Brief

Deloitte in Singapore wanted a functional installation in their reception as a practical decoration that was also representative of the organisation's continuing efforts to adopt technology and embrace the future.

Solution

Working closely together with Deloitte in Singapore, we designed an entire wall panel that is integrated with a large touchscreen. Together with the client, we curated interactive and educational content, powered by a CMS system, for the reception interactive wall that was delivered in spectacular Deloitte fashion.
