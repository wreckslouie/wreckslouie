Deloitte in Singapore 50th Year Anniversary Celebration

1/5

In 2017, Deloitte in Singapore, a professional services and consultancy giant turned fifty. To commemorate the occasion, Deloitte in Singapore wished for an event that is meaningful and true to their people, clients, and the society.

Obstacles

Deloitte in Singapore was having difficulty establishing representative and integral elements from their three core pillars - Client, People, Society. In addition to ascertaining those elements, Deloitte in Singapore needed to develop overall synergy.

Response

We broke the key elements into two parts, i.e. “Client and People”, and “Society.”

We applied “Gramercy” to the Client and People in the form of a black-tie banquet at the historical venue of Clifford Pier.

We intricately planned literary that integrates Society.

The Programmes

Have celebrity local dessert chef deliver an extraordinary Chocolate art piece that commemorated Deloitte in Singapore’s 50th Anniversary.

Chose Diana Ser as the evening’s host.

Lined up local talents such as the T’ang Quartet, and Clement Chow, and the talented students from North Light.

The Gifts

Children of the Metta Welfare Association got involved in creating meaningful gifts for the esteemed guest of the event. They moulded clay into medallions engraved with the mandarin character of “trust.”

Outcome

Influenz managed to uphold Deloitte in Singapore’s brand values and integrity. Overall, the event was a huge success filled with meaning and purpose. Through Deloitte in Singapore’s 50th Anniversary event, new bonds were forged while some were renewed.
