Event Show Brochures and Leaflets Design and Production

THAIFEX - World of Food Asia 2018 Tradeshow Brochures

Brief

The agency was tasked to create the 2018 tradeshow brochure for THAIFEX while integrating the new brand image. The client also identified that the information within could be better organised.

Solution

Through a complete redesign, the 2018 tradeshow brochure and leaflet presented a clear and revamped brand identity. The brochures also included clear illustrations that delivered multi-level statistics in simple and digestible info-graphics.
