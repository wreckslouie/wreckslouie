Digital Product Brochure Design

YTS Product Brochures

Brief

YTS is a giant in the corporate gifting sector. Due to its early start and the rampant development of new technology, YTS needed a total aesthetic revamp of its brochures along with an update of the products.

Solution

Instead of developing a giant all-in-one brochure, the team took to segregating the top gifts into three categories. The audience of YTS was then able to quickly download the brochures of their specific interest and review their products of interest. We also made sure to present the new brochures with a stylish and practical layout to reflect YTS's trendy corporate brand image.
