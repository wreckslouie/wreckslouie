Storyboarding and Video Production

Deloitte in Singapore and EDB Memorandum of Understanding Signing Ceremony

Brief

To commemorate the significant event between Deloitte in Singapore and the EDB, the client required a short animated video that was scheduled to play as part of the signing ceremony.

Solution

Working together with the client, we took to understanding Deloitte in Singapore's vision of “Future of Transport”, “Future of Work”, and “Future of Cognitive Risk”. The result was a short animated video that briefly illustrated key points of those aforementioned.
