Deloitte in Singapore 50th Anniversary Commemorative Book

Brief

As the top professional consultancy firm in Singapore, Deloitte in Singapore was to celebrate their 50th Anniversary in 2017. Our task was to commemorate the history and progress of the organisation through the years with a history and vision-filled commemorative book that shows appreciation to all their staff and partners over the years.

Solution

We got to work first by understanding and then sorting out the historical facts of Deloitte in Singapore. Together with the Deloitte, we then managed to conceive the perfect story and content flow. Next, we to go through a hundred different papers that would become the inside pages, a dozen various methods that would be the binding method and an array of other factors. Ultimately our style and design decisions delivered a commemorative book that was representative of the Anniversary’s significance.
