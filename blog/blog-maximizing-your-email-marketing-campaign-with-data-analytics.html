“You've got mail.”

Such a legendary phrase. Who would have thought that such a simple notification from AOL about receiving an email could change the course of history? I mean, they made a Hollywood movie starring Tom Hanks and Meg Ryan titled, 'You've got mail'.

Email has come a long way since its first inception under the AOL banner. A tool used by business folk who needed to communicate quickly between each other and amongst colleagues in office. Today, email is more than just a means of communication, emails have become a means of business.

From awareness programs, educational content, shopping catalogs and promotional offers, email does it all.

Marketers know the value of email marketing but not many fully understand how to make full use of email marketing. A familiar problem that many marketers face is the problem of email ROI (Return on Investment). It is hard to justify the effort sometimes.

According to Jetlore, there is a simple way of understanding email marketing ROI. Much like bottom of the funnel marketing practices, email marketing has a funnel system too. Of course, with every method, there will always be a way to calculate the revenue but as with all things, one method will never fit every campaign perfectly.

Marketers always argue about the value of email marketing. Yes, it might seem like the easier means of reaching out to an audience but for the longest times, not everyone knew how to translate that email into leads and finally into customers.

If you were to ask some of the top marketers and business owners what they thought of email marketing, they will probably all tell you the same thing. Email marketing is great. But what exactly is so great about it? Of the many positives and important facts about email marketing, the top things to note in mail marketing is; compelling content, engaging emails and most importantly, understanding the customer and being personal with them.

What you put into your email is extremely important but knowing how to analyse that emails reach and impact it has, is a totally different thing. There are many software tools out there that can translate your email into data and Hubspot is no different. According to them, there are 6 Email Metrics that we all should know, and it will make a difference in the way we look at email marketing.

Email marketing might not be something new but by no means is it out dates or obsolete. Every day, marketers are finding new ways to monopolize the use of email marketing to maximize their revenue. Don't get left behind.
