THAIFEX - World of Food Asia Digital Revamp

Brief

Koelnmesse's THAIFEX - World of Food Asia is the leading F&B tradeshow for global businesses to penetrate into the ASEAN market. The THAIFEX website having left to the element of time, was dated and required a revamp that would align itself with contemporary design cues and achieve better usability for all of its visitors regardless of their device.

Solution

The team delivered a design that allowed efficient information dissemination. Along with an elegant and modern design, the team integrated multiple advertising spaces that permitted sponsorship opportunities without compromising design. Among many things, the website achieved distinct compartmentalisation of its information through functional design within its pages. The website is also responsive to a variety of screen sizes which accommodates different devices.
