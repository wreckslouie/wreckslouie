Deloitte 50th Anniversary Gift Design and Procurement

Brief

Drawing inspiration from the organisation’s business, the proposed gift idea took the form of a Chinese Chess Set. It symbolises strategy, tactic, and careful planning, which are the vital elements in their continuing success. To enrich the gift further, we included children of the Metta Welfare Association to hand-mould and create clay medallions engraved with the mandarin character of “Trust.” The result was a gift to every guest at the event that was procured through thought and inclusiveness and has an overall symbolism of trust in the strategy and planning of Deloitte in Singapore.
